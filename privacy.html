Privacy Policy

Last updated July 18, 2026

Information you add

Intentional stores information you enter, including tasks, notes, meeting details, checklists, selected people or departments, photos, and file attachments.

iCloud sync and sharing

Tasks, task lists, task notes, and task attachments sync through your private iCloud account when iCloud is available. A task list is shared with another iCloud user only when you explicitly use the list-sharing feature. Shared-list participants can view or modify records included in that share according to the permission selected by the list owner.

Meeting details, checklists, people directories, templates, and app settings are stored on your device and do not sync between devices.

Device permissions

With your permission, Intentional can access calendars to display and create meetings, Reminders to synchronize lists you select, the camera and photo library to attach images, and files you select as attachments. These permissions remain under your control in Apple Settings.

Tracking and advertising

Intentional does not operate an advertising, analytics, or tracking service. Intentional does not sell your information.

Retention and deletion

Information remains until you delete it in Intentional, remove a shared list, leave a share, or delete it through the applicable Apple service. Cloud changes can remain temporarily while an offline device waits to synchronize. You can revoke device permissions in Settings and manage iCloud data through your Apple account.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the Intentional support page.
