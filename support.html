Intentional Support

Help with tasks, meetings, journals, checklists, and people sources.

Tasks are not appearing on another device

Confirm both devices use the same Apple Account, iCloud Drive is enabled, and Intentional is allowed to use iCloud. Keep Intentional open briefly on each device so pending task and attachment changes can finish syncing.

Meetings or checklists are not appearing on another device

Meeting details, checklists, people directories, templates, and app settings are stored on the device where they were created. Tasks and task lists are the records that sync through iCloud.

Calendar events are missing

Open Intentional settings, review the selected meeting calendars, and confirm Calendar access is enabled for Intentional in Apple Settings.

People or departments are missing

Open People Source settings to refresh a linked file or Intentional Coverage. You can also add people and departments manually.

Delete data

Delete individual tasks or lists inside Intentional. To remove device-only data, delete the applicable meeting, checklist, directory, or template in the app. iCloud task changes may remain queued until the device reconnects.

Contact support

Report a problem through the public Intentional support issue tracker. Do not include private task content, photos, files, associate IDs, or schedules in a public report.
